Porter has put the temporary **$40 CAD fuel surcharge** back on new VIPorter reward flight bookings. The airline had cut that fee to $20 CAD in June while fuel prices eased, then raised it again for bookings made as of July 17th, 2026, after another jump in jet fuel costs. Cash tickets that do not use points are not charged this reward surcharge, and existing award bookings keep whatever rate you already paid.

## What changed

| Item | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Who pays | New VIPorter **reward** bookings |
| Amount | **$40 CAD** fuel surcharge (back from $20 CAD) |
| When | New bookings as of **July 17th, 2026** |
| Protected | Award bookings already ticketed keep their original rate |
| Cash fares | No separate VIPorter fuel surcharge on regular paid tickets |

Porter told members the move tracks another sharp rise in global jet fuel prices, and that it still plans to remove the surcharge when market conditions allow. [Porter’s fees page](https://www.flyporter.com/en-ca/book-travel/our-fares-and-fees/fees-taxes-charges) already notes that fuel can be included in NAV-style surcharges and may change without much notice. On redemptions, [VIPorter terms](https://www.flyporter.com/en-ca/viporter/terms-and-conditions) also make clear that points cover **base fare** only. Taxes and surcharges are paid in cash.

## What this means for travelers

If you redeem VIPorter points for a Porter flight now, budget **about $40 CAD extra** beyond taxes and other fees. A simple round trip booked as two award segments can mean two surcharges. Points can still be a good deal, but the all-in award cost changed, so [compare the points-plus-cash total to a normal cash fare](https://pointsorcash.com/) before you hit confirm.

Holding a BMO VIPorter credit card does **not** waive this surcharge. The card is still the main way Canadians earn VIPorter points and unlock Porter travel perks, but the fee sits on the award booking itself.

## How to earn more VIPorter points (and other useful Porter benefits)

If you fly Porter enough for points and bags to matter, the strongest everyday path is the [BMO VIPorter World Elite Mastercard](https://flytrippers.com/best-credit-cards-canada/bmo-viporter-world-elite-mastercard/?ftpid=1006&sid=TNR). It earns VIPorter points on spending, and cardholders get automatic VIPorter Venture-level benefits such as free checked bag and full-size carry-on for you and companions on the same Porter booking, free standard seat selection, and priority lines. Full current bonus, income, and fee details live on that page.

## Bottom line

Porter’s temporary VIPorter fuel surcharge is back at **$40 CAD** for new award bookings. Before you redeem, add up the cash fees you’ll still pay. Already-ticketed awards keep the rate you locked in.
